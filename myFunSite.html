Cupcake Galore

by Narissa

The number 1 store for cupcakes around the world.

Cupcake Galore is a small and individual business that started up in 2010 in Seattle, Washington. The name Cupcake Galore represents how the cupcakes are presented and sold.

Even though Cupcake Galore started off as a small store in the middle of Seattle, I started to expand the business to other States in America, which led to the expansion of the busness in other continents.

Crazy for Cupcakes!

The slogan for the company is "Crazy for Cupcakes" and this is because as a child I would go CRAZY for cupcakes. For my birthday, I would never have an actual cake, but I would have dozens of cupcakes instead.
